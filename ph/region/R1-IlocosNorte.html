Ilocos Norte

  • Location: Ilocos Norte is a province located in the Ilocos Region of the Philippines, situated in the northwestern part of Luzon island.
  • Overview:  Known for its rich history and scenic landscapes, Ilocos Norte offers a blend of cultural heritage, natural wonders, and warm hospitality. It is home to beautiful beaches, historical sites, and vibrant festivals.
  • Population: Ilocos Norte has a population of over 593,000 residents, comprising both rural and urban communities.
